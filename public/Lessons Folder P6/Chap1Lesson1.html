Lesson 1: Introduction to Physics

____________________________________________________________________________________________________________


Humans have always been fascinated by the mechanics of objects. Not always because of curiosity, but also

because of necessity. Ancient civiliations set the ground work for all modern physics. The Sumerians created

a timekeeping and weight measurement systems that play a vital role in calculations. Many developments in

the modern worl are built on these ancient foundations, and all discoveries are based on them. Physics is

the study of matter, energy, and their interactions. It seeks to understand the fundamental principles that

govern the universe. Hence the goal of this short course, to impart on you the core of our moden world.
