Lesson 2: Free Body Diagrams

____________________________________________________________________________________________________________


Free body diagrams are graphical representations used in physics to visualize all the forces acting on a single object. They are essential tools for analyzing the motion of objects and solving problems in mechanics. It is great help in problem-solving, helping you identify the direction of forces and which of them are diagonal. The picture below is an example of one:
